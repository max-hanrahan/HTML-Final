I'm in a Band Now

      Well, actually, I've been practicing and involved in the writing process for about a year now, but our first show has finally taken place! We are Sounds Like A Plan (conveniently abreviate-able as S.L.A.P.), and you can watch our first show on YouTube.
Our band is a unique blend of punk, prog, jazz, and R&B. My brother and I have teamed up with vocalist Robert Fox, supplying our guitar skills alongside bassist Blake Peterson and drummer Mike Wolfe. We're on a mission to save Erie's disintegrating music scene, and we'll be playing a lot of shows soon. Next stop: January 12 at Basement Transmissions. Be there!


My Cross Country Experience

      Five years ago, I had no athletic ability whatsoever. I needed a hobby and a resume stuffer, so I decided to try out cross country. It was a difficult transition, and I think my first 5k took me 39 minutes to complete. Fast forward to today: I have stayed on the varsity team for three straight years, recieved the "Most Dedicated Teammate" award twice, and served as the first ever team captain of the Erie High Royals.

I am now a QuestBridge Finalist!

     If I may hop on here just to humblebrag, I have accomplisghed something rare and prestigious. In 2017, only 5759 applicants became National College Match Finalists, out of 15606 applicants. Although I wasn't among the lucky few to be "matched" (which is what I was hoping for), it is still something that I am particularly proud of. This is one of the greatest achievements that I have ever accomplished, and I just want to thank my family, my classmates, and my teachers for helping me garnish this rare opportunity.
