Academic Background

I am a senior at Collegiate Academy. I am in the top 15% of my class, and have participated in National Honor Society (Scholarship Committee), Peer Tutoring, Latin club, the PA Junior Academy of Sciences, Math Club, and Mathematical Proofs Club. Click here to see my full resume.
I have applied to sixteen colleges and universities (which is way too many, apply to eight at the most, seniors!), and as of now, I've heard back from seven. Gannon, Penn State, Kent State, Edinboro, and Mercyhurst all admitted me, Colorado College deferred me, and the University of Chicago denied me.



Honestly, I'm not too surprised (or sad) about that last one.


I am also a QuestBridge Finalist, which was part of my motivation to apply to so many colleges. No matter where I go, I hope to use my passion for mathematics to better the world through the art of problem solving.
